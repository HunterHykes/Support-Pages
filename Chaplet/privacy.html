Chaplet Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the Chaplet iOS application ("the App").

No Collection of Personal Data

The Chaplet app does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. We do not request, access, or track any personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, or location, nor do we collect any non-personal data, such as device information or usage statistics.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that may collect data. All features, including prayers, chaplets, and settings, are provided locally within the App and do not require an internet connection or data transmission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
